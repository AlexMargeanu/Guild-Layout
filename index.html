We are Conundrum. A Draenor based horde guild who are actively recruiting for World of Warcraft: Shadowlands. Our history goes back into the end of Warlords of Draenor on the Genjuros server, where a new guild called Drifters would emerge to conquer all content that Legion had to offer. After clearing the first two raids and taking a brake, Drifters got reorganized into Infinitus, this time changing realm to the Defias Brotherhood RP server due to a lack of population on our home realm. Infinitus is where most of the current members would meet each other and raid, build friendships and even met each other outside of the game as part of annualy held guild meet-ups. Battle for Azeroth would bring us a new chapter in our lives, in which we had to transfer realms again for the same reason and because the guild wanted to be so much more. We eventually switched realms to Draenor in an attempt to find new, like minded players and because the ambitions of the guild had grown.

Who we are

For the first time in our history we are going for Cutting Edge each tier and as such, we are looking for players who know that World of Warcraft is about community. Players who have heard about some guilds meeting up, year after year, and think that forming such a friendship is something they want. We are Conundrum. A Draenor based horde guild who are actively recruiting for World of Warcraft: Shadowlands. We are a group of friends who first met in game and then irl who are looking for like minded players to team up, kill mythic bosses and maybe even make new friends along the way. Our guild was first created four years ago under the name of Drifters on the Genjuros server. After that we migrated to Defias Brotherhood RP server and rebranded ourselves as Infinitus. Our core members didn't know this yet, but this is where they would first meet and the first friendships would be formed. After spending most of Legion there, we migrated yet again to Draenor, this time calling ourselves Conundrum, and we've been here ever since. While clearing HC and Mythic content we have had our ups and downs but the core group as always stayed together. We want to find more people that share our vision and that hopefully want to integrate into our happy little bunch. Hope to see you in discord soon!

We are very grateful for the journey that brought us here but even more so, we are excited to see what lies ahead. Most of us have played this game since day one and we, just like you, have noticed that most guilds only have one focus. If they are doing too well in the raiding scene they probably aren't very friendly. If they are too friendly then that's a tell tale sign that their raiding team is no more than a glorified social guild. We have had our fair share of drama with this aswell. People are very different, some wanna raid mythic, others want to raid heroic, some opt to raid either mythic or heroic but not put in the effort for either of them relying on their guild mates to pick up their role in a fight.

Our Goal

In Shadowlands our vision is to create a guild that is truly capable of offering everything people log into the game for. Want to raid mythic? Sure, we raid 2-3 times a week and we would love to see you there! Want to raid Heroic content? That's great, join our HC only team and have fun! Want to do pet battles, fishing, PvP or any other activity the game has to offer? Awesome! We have a rank just for you so that it's easier to find guild members who share your same passions. All this cannot be accomplished without players and without the right mentality. If you are the type of person who knows that in order to get ahead you have to put in the work, you might be the person we're looking for. If you are willing to put in the effort for every activity you want to do then we would like to meet you. Help us create a one of a kind guild and be a part of our story.

We've never really focused on a set goal within the game, just raided until it wasn't fun anymore or pushed high Mythic+, not because of the Raider.io score or bragging rights but because we were having fun together. No we want to keep that atmosphere while also being determined to achieve more this time around. Our goal for Shadowlands is to get Cutting Edge each raid tier. This is where you come in, dear player, join our ranks, become our friend and help us kill bosses. We don't want just names appearing online and offline again, we are looking for meaningfull relationships with everyone we choose to be at our side so our recruitment methods are adapted to that end. We take into account raiding skills and experience, your type of humor, how active you are on discord and in the game and most important of all: your attitude.

Management

Q : When do you guys raid?

Our raid times and days are Wednesday and Monday from 7 Server time. In order for you to be elligible you must be signed up in the calendar.

Q : Can I also bring a friend to the guild?

If it's just a friend: yes. We are always welcoming to new players, however if we are talking about 'large groups' such as when another guild is disbanding then we will have an officer meeting to decide on our next move.

Q : Anything I need to know if I join you guys?

Yea! Please take time to read the guild rules on Discord and if you're not sure what you want to play, look in the Open Spots section on the site to see what classes are needed and consider picking one of them, we would appreciated it greatly!

Q : I usually don't fit in or don't want to talk. Is that ok?

Yes! No problem on our part, with the following mention: because most people can invoke this aspect of their personality all we ask is just stay on discord, muted and listen. If after a few attempts you still feel the same then by all means just be there for the content.

Q : How do you guys sort BoE's?

You are free to keep your BoE. We use RC loot council if you have a tradeable item that you don't need. In that case management decides which player gets it based on a number of factors.

Q : How do I join you guys?

Easily : check the Join Us section of the website and follow the instructions.

Q : What if I have a problem with one of the guild members or management?

Any issue with our guild members should be brought to the immediate attention of the GM or one of the Officers. If the problem is with anyone from management, talk directly to the GM. If the issue is with the guild master then you can call all 3 officers for a chat with the GM being absent.

Q : What type of content do you guys normally do?

For Shadowlands we will be mainly a raiding guild with the goal of Cutting Edge for each tier. That being said we also do HC raids and allot of M+. Apart from that most of our members do either Arena or RBGs.
